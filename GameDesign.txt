-- Grow Defense! --

- earn money to buy plants
- place plants to defend yourself from enemies
- sometimes get seeds/food
- use it to upgrade/grow your plants

-- Plants --

- Sunflower: Angry / shooter
shoots at the nearest enemy in range

- Mush no Room / splasher
always shoots
shoots in circular area around it ( like waves )

- Wood, just wood / wall
just tanks damage from enemies

- chill guy, daisy / slower
slows enemies

- lily the aura buffer / support
buffs nearby plants, doesn't stack

- mighty watermelon / mortar
slow attack speed, high area damage

-- Enemies --

- basic
stupid as f

- fast
moves fast but is low hp

- tanky
moves slow but is high hp

- ranged
can attack from range, low hp

- bomb
moves fast, deals a lot of damage, explodes on dead, low hp

- boss
slow, high hp, spawns other enemies
